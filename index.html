The People Behind the Lens

We are Moments.

We started photographing weddings because we're endlessly moved by the unguarded moments — a father's quiet tears, the nervous laughter before the pheras, grandmothers dancing like nobody's watching.

Over the last nine years and 250+ weddings across India, our small team has learned to disappear into the day so the moments stay real. We shoot warm, we shoot honest, and we deliver heirlooms — not just files.
